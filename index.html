Welcome

The Department of Computer Science was established during 2013-2014 with 3 staff members. At present there are more than 7 staff members. Our Department was started with 34 students on 2013, Now the cs department is valued more than its prime value with Postgraduate students. We have 50 computers with 3 laser printers, 2 dot matrix printer, high-end photo copier machine and we have fastest internet connection. Our department library gives unlimited access to more than 530 computer related books, which is very helpful for the student's research. The students of computer science are permitted to attend various programmes inside and outside the college and we also conduct sports week every year. Our college is recognized and affiliated by Bharathiyar University, so we follow the syllabus is recommended by the Bharathiyar University.
